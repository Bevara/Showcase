Streaming of VR/360 content

This demo uses a non-standard content within the MPEG-4 Part 14 container: a 360 content handled by WebCodecs and displayed on an interactive canvas. This page is embedded in the MP4 and is opened by a service worker.

In most browsers, WebCodecs support is experimental or unavailable. This demo works best on Google Chrome starting from version 94.
